/ How it works

Four steps. Five minutes.

From scrap to perfect settings in less time than it takes to brew coffee.

01
Measure

Cut a scrap piece

Use the same material you're calibrating for. 60×60mm is plenty for most tests.

02
Configure

Set the ranges

Enter card size and the power/speed limits to bracket. 100–1000 power and 1000–3000 mm/min works for most diode lasers.

03
Generate

Download .nc

Click the live preview to save G-code. Load it in LaserGRBL, frame the job, and run.

04
Read

Pick the cell

Find the lightest cell with a clean, fully-burnt mark. The power and speed are labeled right on the card.

/ Interpretation

Reading the burn.

Three outcomes. Only one is the answer.

↓ Underburnt

Too little

Faint, broken, or absent mark. Power too low or speed too high. Move down or right on the grid.

★ Sweet spot

Optimal

Clean, uniform, fully-burnt without scorching. The lightest cell that still hits this is your number.

↑ Overburnt

Too much

Charring, blow-through, smoke residue. Power too high or speed too low. Back off.
